# Vision

AMPE is not a generic factor screener. It is a research engine that estimates **what characteristics the market is currently rewarding**, explains the evidence, and then ranks securities using those inferred priorities.

The intended experience is zero-operation for the owner:

> “Run the Market Priority Report.”

The system should automatically acquire data, validate it, compute the research outputs, preserve an audit trail, and return a clear report. The AI interface explains outputs; it must not invent calculations.

## Core outputs
- Current market priorities and uncertainty
- Factor-level evidence and trend
- FDR-controlled factor × macro interactions
- Changes from the prior rebalance
- Top-ranked securities and factor attribution
- Data-quality and audit disclosures
- Historical analogs and persistence estimates in later phases

## Product principles
- Robustness over complexity
- Point-in-time integrity over convenience
- Economic interpretability over impressive-looking statistics
- Transparent uncertainty over false precision
- Automation without hiding failures
